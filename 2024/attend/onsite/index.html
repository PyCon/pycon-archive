MASK REQUIREMENT

PyCon US continues to keep the safety of our community as our top priority and wants PyCon US to be an event that everyone feels safe attending. With that being said, PyCon US is requiring masks to be worn by all participants while at the conference. All attendees of PyCon US 2024 are required to comply with these Guidelines for the duration of the event. Any person violating the Health and Safety Guidelines may be asked to leave the event.

REGISTRATION

Registration will open at 7:00 am EST on Wednesday, May 15th.

When arriving, please enter through the East Entrance (facing 10th Street). Head up the escalators to Concourse A to find the registration check-in counter. Please note that you must check in and have your badge printed to enter the Expo Hall.

If you are staying at the Westin hotel, you can enter the convention center through the Visitors Walkway, found on the second floor. Once inside the convention center, take the elevator to the 3rd floor and head to Concourse A to find registration.

Please also note that the registration desk will NOT be open during the post-conference Sprint days (Monday, May 20th-Thursday, May 23rd). If you are planning to attend any of the Sprint days, you MUST check-in before Sprints start at the conference registration desk, which closes at 1 PM EST on Sunday, May 19th.

Please maintain social distancing and have your mask on while standing in the queue. Have your ID ready when standing in the queue.

Hours:

  • Wednesday, May 15: 7:00 am - 6:00 pm EST
  • Thursday, May 16: 7:00 am - 6:00 pm EST
  • Friday, May 17: 7:00 am - 5:00 pm EST
  • Saturday, May 18: 8:00 am - 5:00 pm EST
  • Sunday, May 19: 8:00 am - 1:00 pm EST

DAVID L. LAWRENCE CONVENTION CENTER

The David L. Lawrence Convention Center is located at 1000 Fort Duquesne Boulevard, Pittsburgh, PA 15222. We recommend all attendees enter through the East Entrance on 10th Street.

Attendees staying at the Westin Hotel can enter the convention center through the Westin Hotel Visitor Corridor.

  • David L. Lawrence Convention Center Floorplan
  • Directions and Parking Information

NURSING SUITE

Located on the second level by Hall B (next to the glass elevator), the DLCC offers a dedicated space for parents of newborns/infants to be used as a private space. When at the DLCC, please ask a staff member for the location and how to gain access.

GENDER NEUTRAL RESTROOMS

Several gender-neutral restrooms can be found throughout the convention center. You can find each gender-neutral restroom marked with a red “X” via this map here.

LUGGAGE CHECK

Luggage check will be available at the convention center in Show Office C on Saturday, May 18th and Sunday, May 19th from 8:00 am - 6:00 pm EST.

All items must be picked up by 6:00 pm EST on both days. The room will be locked at 6:00 pm EST. If you miss the pickup window, please come to the Staff Office (Room 306/307).
