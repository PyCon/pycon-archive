# Free Sponsor Workshops PyCon 2016 is proud to work with our amazing sponsors to bring to you a bevy of **free** one-and-a-half hour & three hour workshops during the PyCon tutorial days. They are great to enjoy stand-alone, or as an add-on to paid tutorials you want to attend. If you are a sponsor and would like to give a sponsor tutorial, please [contact us](mailto:pycon-sponsors@python.org)! - **When**: Saturday, May 28th,2016 & Sunday, May 29th, 2016 - **Where**: Oregon Convention Center - **Room**: Rooms A106 & A107-A109 - **Cost**: FREE! - **Register**: [Registration is open](https://us.pycon.org/2016/registration/register/) - **Includes**: AM Break, Lunch, PM Break
