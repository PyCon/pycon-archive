# Tutorials This is the full schedule of tutorials for PyCon 2016. See also: * [Full list of tutorials, with abstracts](/2016/schedule/tutorials/list/) * [Overview of conference schedule](/2016/events/schedule) Each PyCon tutorial is 3 hours of instruction plus a 20 minute break in the middle.
