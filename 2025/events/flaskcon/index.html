Hatchery: FlaskCon

Join us in a mini conference dedicated to Flask, its community and ecosystem, as well as related web technologies. Meet maintainers and community members, learn about how to get involved, and join us during the sprint days to contribute.

FlaskCon 2025 will be happening Friday, May 16 inside PyCon US 2025 in Room 317.

More information can be found on the FlaskCon website, we look forward to seeing you there!

https://flaskcon.com/2025/

Organized by David Lord, Phil Jones, Adam Englander, David Carmichael, Abdur-Rahmaan Janhangeer

Schedule
Time Event
14:00 - 14:30 Testing Flask and Quart Apps with Pytest and Playwright - Pamela Fox
14:45 - 15:15 Turning Data into an Interactive Artwork That Tells a Story - Diane Phan
15:30 - 16:00 Python’s New Template Strings — And Flask - Paul Everitt
16:00 - 16:30 Break
16:30 - 17:00 Let's PyScript: Flask frontends in Python! - Nicholas Tollervey & Paul Everitt
17:15 - 17:45 Death to the spinner: event sourcing for reactive web apps - Chris May

If you can't make it on the day it would be great to see you at the PyCon sprints through Monday, May 19th to Thursday, May 22nd.
