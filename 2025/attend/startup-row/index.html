At PyCon US, we reserve a special place for startup companies. Startup Row companies receive complimentary conference passes, booth space on Thursday evening for the Opening Reception (May 15, 2025) as well as on Friday and Saturday of the main conference (May 16 - 17, 2025), access to the Job Fair (May 18, 2025), and all the best of what PyCon US has to offer. Does your startup qualify? Here are the criteria:

  • You use Python somewhere (anywhere, but the more the better) in your tech or business stack
  • Your startup is 2.5 years old, or less, at time of applying (if you had a major pivot or delayed product launch, measure from there)
  • You have 25 or fewer full-time employees, including founders, at the time of applying
  • You or your company will fund travel and accommodation to PyCon US in Pittsburgh, PA
  • You have not previously presented your current company on Startup Row at PyCon US (Previous applicants and alumni founders working on new projects are welcome to apply!)
  • You are able to attend and staff your booth on the following days:
    • Thursday, May 15, 2025: Opening Reception
    • Friday, May 16 & Saturday, May 17, 2025: Main conference expo hall days
  • Please note: if your company is selected to be featured on Startup Row, a $400 USD refundable deposit is required to secure your booth space and Job Fair table (if opting-in to attend the Job Fair). If all events are attended and staffed by your company, the $400 deposit will be refunded after the event.
