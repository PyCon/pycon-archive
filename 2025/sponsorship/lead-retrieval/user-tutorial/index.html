User Tutorial

Lead Retrieval Users will have been invited by a Lead Retrieval Manager for a given team.

Once invited, Lead Retrieval Users may:

  • Access the Lead Retrieval scanning app inside the PyCon US Mobile App
  • Access the Lead Retrieval dashboard on us.pycon.org, with the ability to search and review their own captured leads.

To get started, download the app for your iOS or Android device from the appropriate store:

  • PyCon US Mobile on Apple App Store
  • PyCon US Mobile on Google Play Store

Next, all you need to do is open the app and sign-in with your us.pycon.org account! You'll see the "Lead Retrieval" tab in the lower and navigation menus.
