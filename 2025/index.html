Presenting PyCon US 2025

May 14 - May 22, 2025

We are so excited to welcome our community back to the wonderful city of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania for PyCon US 2025! Mark your calendars and be sure to read the About PyCon US page for more details. We can’t wait to see you all at the David L. Lawrence Convention Center!

Registration is Open!

PyCon US registration is now open!

More information can be found on the Registration Information page.

Please note: PyCon US 2025 will take place fully in-person in Pittsburgh, PA with no live streaming of the main conference this year. More information can be found via our blog here.

PyCon US continues to keep the safety of our community as our top priority and wants PyCon US to be an event that everyone feels safe attending. With that in mind, PyCon US 2025 will have Health and Safety Guidelines in place again this year. For full details, read the PyCon US 2025 Health & Safety Guidelines here.

As we work to finalize our plans, we will keep you informed with updates on the exciting developments. In the meantime, our staff and volunteers are working to bring you a fun, energizing event filled with networking, tutorials, in-person collaboration, social events, and so much more!

To stay informed on details and announcements be sure to create an account here on this website and opt-in to receive PyCon US News. Also, follow us on Twitter and Mastodon and subscribe to the PyCon US Blog.
