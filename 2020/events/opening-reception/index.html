Opening Reception

Come kick off PyCon, on the evening that tutorials wrap up and everyone is looking forward to the first day of talks.

Join us for the opening reception in the Expo Hall!

Light food will be provided, as well as a free drink ticket and a cash bar.

Who: PyCon Attendees
When: Thursday, April 16, 5:30pm - 7:30pm
Where: David L. Lawrence Convention Center - Hall A

Register: Your conference registration is your ticket to the event - just show your badge at the door.
A limited number of Guest Opening Reception tickets will be available for purchase for $30.
Register your guest when you register as an attendee.
