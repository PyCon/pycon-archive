8th Annual PyLadies Lunch

Thanks again to the generous support of the PSF and PyCon organizers, PyLadies will be having a luncheon for women on the third day of the conference!

In order to help the organizers plan the event, we highly recommend you procure your free ticket now, via the RSVP link below.

The PyLadies Lunch is a great opportunity for women attending the conference who love Python, or who want to learn more about Python or PyLadies, to meet others in the same boat in a fun, relaxed environment.

We cordially invite you to join us for updates on the PyLadies Organization, make new friends, forge connections, and share Python programming stories with folks from all around the world.

Who: Anyone who identifies as a woman who is attending PyCon
When: Sunday, April 19th, 2020, 12pm - 1pm (during regular conference lunch)
Where: David L. Lawrence Convention Center, Room 404-405
RSVP: Eventbrite - Free but exclusive to conference attendees.

The PyCon 2020 conference in Pittsburgh, PA, USA, is a production of the Python Software Foundation.
