Session Staff

Volunteer: Please read and understand the duties before you sign up to be a session chair/runner. Follow the links below for complete descriptions.

  • Session Chairs and Session Runners are present for an entire session (usually 2 to 3 talks in a row).
  • A Session Chair introduces the speaker, manages the time, and facilitates the question and answer period. “Please be seated. Our next speaker is…”
  • A Session Runner accompanies the speaker from the Speakers Lounge to the appropriate stage. They help in any way needed to make the session run smoothly. No running required.
  • You must be logged in to view the sign ups on https://us.pycon.org/2020/schedule/talks/


Green Room Staff

Volunteer: Sign up to help for a talk block

  • Green room staff help the Session Staff Chairs coordinate keeping the talks on schedule. They check in volunteers & speakers, keep an eye on the clock, use a walkie-talkie, and reassure speakers before their talk.


Registration Desk

Volunteer: Sign-up for an hour slot at the registration desk

  • Someone needs to say “Welcome! What is your name?”, print a name tag, hand them their stuff.
  • Please consider signing up for an hour on the registration desk. It’s a great way to meet people and answer basic questions. If this is your first PyCon, it’s a good way to get into volunteering!


Swag Bag Handout

Volunteer: Sign-up for an hour slot at the swag/t-shirt desk

  • Help distributing swag bags & t-shirts; especially during that busy first morning of the conference.


Tutorial Support

Volunteer: Sign-up for an hour slot helping with a tutorial

  • We’re looking for one or two “Tutorial Hosts” for each tutorial to help welcome participants as they arrive: be sure they’re in the right tutorial, give them any printed handouts, ask them if they need help setting up their laptop, etc.

Stuff Swag Bags

Volunteer: Just show up! Thursday April 16, 1pm - 4pm (or until we’re done)
