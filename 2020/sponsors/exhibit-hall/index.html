Exhibit Hall

The Exhibit Hall will be located in Hall A in the David H. Lawrence Convention Center.

The opening reception is in the expo hall, in addition to breakfasts, all breaks, and lunches.

Beverages and lunch will be available in the “Sponsor Lounge” in the expo hall.

Please prepare to have your booths staffed from 8:00am until 5pm, and during the opening reception. It is mandatory that your booth is staffed for the “active times” (see below).

Move-in for the Exhibit Hall
* Keystone & Diamond Level: begins on Tuesday, April 14 at 2:00pm - 7:00pm and concludes on Thursday, April 16 at 12:00pm.
* Platinum & Gold Levels: begins on Wednesday, April 15 at 8:00am - 6:00pm and concludes on Thursday, April 16 at 12:00pm.
* There is an Opening Reception on the Exhibit Floor from 5:30pm to 7:30pm on Thursday.

Exhibit Floor Times

  • Thursday, April 16, 2020 – 5:30pm to 7:30pm (Opening Reception)

  • Friday, April 17, 2020 – 8:00am to 5:00pm
    Active times are:

    • 8:00am until 9:30am
    • 10:10am until 10:50am
    • 12:40pm until 1:55pm
    • 3:45pm until 4:30pm

  • Saturday, April 18, 2020 – 8:00am to 5:00pm
    Active times are:

    • 8:00am until 9:30am
    • 10:10am until 10:50am
    • 12:40pm until 1:55pm
    • 3:45pm until 4:30pm

Move-out for the Exhibit hall booths begins at 5:00pm Saturday, April 18. Everything needs to be cleared out by 7pm. At this point, we transform the Expo Hall into the Job Fair (details for the job fair below).

Note: You have an option to opt-out of the Booth Space in the Exhibit Hall. However, if you do not opt-out before the start of the conference and leave your booth empty, a fee will be applied to your sponsorship. This can be arranged by contacting us at pycon-sponsors@python.org.

Job Fair Information

In previous years, we have had an amazing turn out at our Job Fair. Many companies found good prospects during the three hour event, some receiving hundreds of resumes in a short time.

The job fair will be open to the public. Tickets will be available online. This opens up new opportunities to reach the local dev scene, schools, and user groups, and increases the Job Fair audience with quality candidates.

  • Date & Time
    Sunday, April 19, 2020 - 10:00am to 1:00pm

  • Location
    Exhibit Hall A - David H. Lawrence Convention Center.
    The Job Fair is open to attendees and the public from 10:00am to 1:00pm (3 hours).
    AM Break and Lunch will be served during the Job Fair.

  • Move-in
    Begins at 8:00am Sunday, April 19, 2020

  • Move-out
    Begins at 1:00pm. Sponsor material should be cleared out by 3:00pm.

  • Layout
    Every participant will have a six foot table and chairs set aside for them; we do not use booths during the Job Fair. You are welcome to have a standing banner or customized table cloth. Give aways are also welcome. Conference wifi and standard power will be provided.

Note: The Expo Hall with booths is a different event than the Job Fair. We do not reuse the Expo Hall booths for the job fair, the entire room is reset Saturday, April 18, 2020, beginning at 5:00pm. Please opt in for the Job Fair during sign up.
