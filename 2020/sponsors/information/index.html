Sponsor Registration and Housing

Registration voucher codes will be issued to sponsor contacts. Please go to your dashboard where you can find your voucher code details. For those that have not yet booked hotel rooms or did not receive their voucher codes, please contact our registration team for assistance at pycon-reg@python.org

Sponsor Lead Retrieval

This year we’re doing something different for lead generation. We’re working with Boomset to offer a new mobile application for 2020 that can be used with your booth staff’s own mobile device on Android or iOS. The cost is $150.00 per license that allows for multiple team members to be signed into or you can purchase additional licenses if needed. More information can be found here: https://tinyurl.com/qpdnjlf

PyCon Internet

We as a conference provide wifi in all of the conference spaces including the expo. You are welcome to use this wifi at no additional cost, but it is shared with all of the attendees. If you wish to order your own dedicated Internet, please see the David L. Lawrence Convention Center Exhibitor Ordering Guide - http://www.pittsburghcc.com/exhibitors/exhibitor-services/

The advanced warehouse is open to receive PyCon packages from March 16, 2020 - April 8, 2020, Monday - Friday from 7:30am to 3:30pm.

Important Exhibition Information

The Exhibit Hall is located in Exhibit Hall A in the David L. Lawrence Convention Center. It is near Registration and in close proximity to all the sessions. Keep in mind that the expo floor opens for the Opening Reception at 5:30 pm Thursday, April 16, 2020. Please have your booth ready and staffed for the event.

The expo floor with booths continues until 5:00 pm Saturday, April 18, 2020. Please use the appropriate shipping labels for your EXHIBITION FREIGHT in the exhibitor kit. We kindly ask that you do not pack swag bag inserts with your exhibition freight since we stuff bags before you get to your booth for setup. Please ship those items separately with the appropriate shipping label. For your booth items, use the advanced shipping labels in the exhibitor kit.

Note: Beverages and lunch will be available in the “Sponsor Lounge” in the expo hall Please prepare to have your booths staffed from 8:00 am until 5:00 pm. It is mandatory that your booth is staffed for the “active times” (see below).

Move-in for the Exhibit Hall
* Keystone & Diamond Level: begins on Tuesday, April 14 at 2:00pm - 7:00pm and concludes on Thursday, April 16 at 12:00pm.
* Platinum & Gold Levels: begins on Wednesday, April 15 at 8:00am - 6:00pm and concludes on Thursday, April 16 at 12:00pm.

Job Fair

Link to Job Fair Exhibitor Kit: http://www.tricord.net/assets/2020-pycon---job-fair-kit.pdf

NOTE: Participating sponsors will have a six-foot table and chairs set aside for them; we do not use booths during the Job Fair. Table top spaces are limited to (1) pop-up graphic, branded table cloth, and giveaway items. Carpeting is not an option. Standard power will be provided.

It’s important that job fair boxes be sent with the specific JOB FAIR shipping label in the exhibitor kit, so that they can be delivered right to your table.

The job fair is open to the public. Tickets are available online.

Important Information

  • When & Where: Sunday, April 19, 2020, in Expo Hall A at the David L. Convention Center
  • Time: 10:00am until 1:00pm.
  • Morning refreshments and lunch will be served during the event.
  • Move-in begins at 8:00am Sunday, April 19, 2020
  • Move-out begins at 1:00pm. Sponsor material should be cleared out by 3:00pm.

If you have anything shipped for your Job Fair table that is clearly marked, the boxes will be placed underneath your job fair table.

Accessible Storage

Onsite storage is $75.00 per skid to store. To access the stored items there is a labor fee that is usually a ½ hour. Labor does need to be with the exhibitor when they access so that the trailer can be opened. If there are only a few items to be removed the exhibitor may carry themselves. If boxes need to be moved to the booth area then the labor would need to deliver. See page 16 in the Exhibitor Kit.

Conference Advertisement
