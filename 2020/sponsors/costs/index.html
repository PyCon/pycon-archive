Estimated Sponsor Costs

Below is a breakdown of possible charges a sponsor may incur.

  • Shipping: Sponsors are responsible for shipping charges to and from the conference. Costs depend on carrier and location. Please use Tricord’s online form to request a quote for domestic or international shipping logistics. This will give you a good idea regarding all the costs that are included in shipping to the advance warehouse and/or direct to the convention center.

  • Material Handling: If you choose to ship your items to the advanced warehouse or directly to the convention center, a handling fee will be applied. Handling charges to receive small packages start at $27.00. Handling charges for crated material (on a skid or pallet) start at $133.00 per 100 lbs. It is more economical to ship this way, rather than ship individual boxes.

  • [Optional] Material storage: for extra items that you will need to bring out (t-shirts or other giveaways) that you don’t have room for in your booth. Accessible Storage is available onsite. The cost for this service is $75.00 per skid or pallet to store plus (1/4) hour of labor for each access. Please visit the Service Desk for labels and storage request instructions.

  • [Optional] Wifi: standard conference wifi is included - Up to 2Mbps for 1 device. Shared wifi network: Prices range from a “run of show” (all three days) of $695 to $2,640, depending on the number of devices and Mbps (early bird pricing). Private bandwidth wired service is $3495 to $7850.

  • [Optional] Carpet: We recommend having a carpet and pad in your booth. It looks great and provides a comfortable surface for your staff and attendees to stand on. Package pricing for pre-cut carpet and pad start at (early bird pricing deadline March 24, 2020) $175 for a 10x10 booth and $345 for a 10 x 20 booth. See pages 25-27 in the exhibitor kit.

  • [Optional] Booth cleaning: Vacuuming a 10x10 booth is $25/day; 10x20 is $50/day; 20x20 is $100/day; 20x30 is $150 (early bird pricing for all days - deadline March 24, 2020). You can also bring your own vacuum and do it yourself.

  • [Optional] Custom and turnkey booths: see pages 22-24 in the exhibitor kit.
