SLC Events During PyCon US

PROGRAMER SPECIFIC

  • April 21, 2023: Join AWS and Superblocks as they host their ‘PyParty’ at Squatters Pub! Please ensure you wear your PyCon badge.
  • April 24, 2023: The Salt Lake Py Bake

MUSEUMS AND CULTURAL

  • April 20 - April 23, 2023: Wasatch Mountain Film Festival
  • April 21, 2023: Scientist in the Spotlight: Paleocologist at the Natural History Museum of Utah
  • April 22, 2023: Paint Night at Hogle Zoo
  • April 25, 2023: Storybook Theater Performance: The Wonderful Wizard of Oz

SPORTS & WELLBEING

  • April 21, 2023: Quality of Life Expo
  • April 22, 2023: Reno Aces vs Salt Lake Bees
  • April 22, 2023: Salt Lake City Marathon

SALT LAKE CITY METROPARKS

  • April 28, 2023: Red Butte Garden: 2023 Arbor Day Celebration
  • April 14 - May 8, 2023: Red Butte Garden: Jeanne Hansen Art Exhibit
