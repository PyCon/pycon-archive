Presenting PyCon US 2023 a 20th Year Anniversary Celebration!

Salt Lake City, Utah - April 19, 2023 - April 27, 2023

  • Tutorials - April 19-20, 2023
  • Main Conference and Online - April 21-23, 2023
  • Job Fair - April 23, 2023
  • Sprints - April 24-27, 2023

The first PyCon on record took place in 2003 so we will be celebrating the past 20 years at PyCon US 2023!

The PyCon US 2023 design is highlighting the past 20 years of PyCon US conferences through the mosaic design which includes small segments of past designs. The emerald green and white are the traditional 20th anniversary colors. Scroll over the mosaic pieces to see which year each mosaic tile represents.

To highlight the past 20 years, we asked you to submit your favorite memories and stories of PyCon US or something you'd hope to learn during your time with us this year. Check out the complete memory recap video highlighting our community members' favorite memories of PyCon US!

To stay informed on details and announcements be sure to create an account here on this website and opt-in to receive PyCon US News and also follow us on Twitter and Mastodon and follow the PyCon US Blog.
