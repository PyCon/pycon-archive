Katherine Scott

Katherine Scott is a freelance computer vision and robotics consultant working with a number of small start-ups to develop open source computer vision solutions to solve some of the world's most pressing problems. Katherine was the lead developer of the SimpleCV computer vision library and co-author of the SimpleCV O'Reilly Book . In her spare time she hacks together robots and teaches kids about robotics and programming.
