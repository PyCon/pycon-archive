PyLadies Auction

We are excited to announce the Tenth PyLadies Charity Auction, which will be in-person during PyCon US 2022! You can have fun and support the PyLadies community while enjoying refreshments. The intention of the PyLadies Auction is to have fun, laugh, raise awareness and funds for PyLadies. So come with a good sense of 'bidding' humor and know that bidding is not required.

  • Saturday, April 30, 2022
  • 6:30pm
  • Salt Lake Marriott Downtown at City Creek Grand Ballroom
    • 75 South West Temple,
    • Salt Lake City, Utah 84101

The Entry cover charge of $35 includes a dinner and free drink ticket, while the Supporter cover charge of $50.00 includes a dinner, free drink ticket, and $15 contribution to PyLadies. Everyone who attends will also have access to the cash bar. To join us at the Auction, add the PyLadies Auction to your PyCon US registration via your dashboard.

The format of the auction will be live bidding only; Utah state does not allow charity raffles.

Sponsors will have the opportunity to donate auction items for the event. If you have any questions about the auction or are interested in participating, please contact our Auction Coordinators. Contributing sponsors will be highlighted in the marketing of the event and also during the event.

Please note that donations of guns, alcohol, cars, real estate, or houses will not be accepted.

PyLadies is an international mentorship group with a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community. Their mission is to promote, educate, and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events, and social gatherings.

PyLadies also aims to provide a friendly support network for women and a bridge to the larger Python world. Anyone with an interest in Python is encouraged to participate! Check out local meetups here: http://www.pyladies.com/locations/.
