PyCon is organized by the Python community **for** the community. It is underwritten by the Python Software Foundation, a 501(c)3 charitable organization set up to manage the growth and development of Python worldwide. Our conference continues to connect with new community members locally, nationally, and globally. Your sponsorship helps keep PyCon affordable and accessible to the widest possible audience. PyCon generates 80% of the PSF’s revenue. Because of your support, we were able to award $118,543 in financial aid to 143 PyCon attendees in 2018 and nearly $200,000 so far in grants to recipients in 34 different countries. Benefits of sponsorship: * Being part of the biggest and most prestigious Python conference in the world * Being matched with those who could potentially become clients or employees * Increasing your brand exposure and elevating your corporate identity within the community * Staying in front of your current and potential customers - 3000+ attendees will see your products * Strengthening your company’s reputation by investing in Python and the open source community Sample sponsorships are below.
